The Survey 2014

We’re an agile team of multidisciplined creative experts who take a strategic approach to deliver results. We produce original and thoughtful pieces of design for businesses throughout Hampshire, London and beyond.

Snowflake is not just another graphic or web design agency. Sure, we spend hours each week keeping up with latest trends and experimenting with new css tricks, but we don’t design for our own portfolio or awards – we design for our customers. What matters more than anything to us and our customers are real, measurable results.

The Panel (morning session)

Decisions and Dilemmas - Finding The Balance.

We love what we do and we’re good at it. It’s not just about picking a pretty colour, or font, aligning some blocks or sticking to rule of third. We will discover the true message of your brand, squeezing out every bit of its potential to deliver a design masterpiece. To us, something is only beautiful & creative if it serves a purpose and solves a problem. Every one of our customers is different and we know how to get the most out of their brand.
